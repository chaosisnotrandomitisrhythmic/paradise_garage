| Spec | Value |
|------|-------|
| Price | ~€1,870 |
| Dimensions | 336 x 410 x 109 mm (W x D x H) |
| Weight | 7 kg |
| Channels | 6+2 |
| Signal path | Fully analog |
| Soundcard | 32-bit, 24-channel USB |

The sound quality winner. Dual HPF/LPF filters per channel, deep sub-bass, 3D soundstage, no harshness. Beats Pioneer DJM-V10 and DJM-A9 in blind listening tests.

### Why Xone:96 over Pioneer?

- **Analog signal path** — no digital conversion in the mix bus
- **Filters** — universally rated as the best in any DJ mixer, smooth resonance
- **Sound** — described as "liberated, cinematic 3D spatiality" vs Pioneer's "compressed upper-mids"
- **6+2 channels** — room to grow (guest deck, drum machine, aux)
- **Price** — €1,870 vs €3,300 for the DJM-V10
